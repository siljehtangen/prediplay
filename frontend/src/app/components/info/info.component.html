How It Works

Understanding the predictions behind every score, pick, and flag

Player Prediction Score (0 – 10)

Every player receives a predicted score representing their expected performance in the upcoming match. Five factors are combined using configurable weights (defaults shown):

Form 35%
The player's average match rating over recent games. Ratings run from 0 – 10; a 7.0+ average is considered solid form. A player on a hot streak scores high here, while poor recent performances drag it down.
Threat 25%
A composite attacking threat score built from: xG × 1.5 + xA × 1.2 + Goals × 0.8 + Assists × 0.6, normalised to 0 – 10. Reflects both actual returns and underlying expected contributions.
Opponent Difficulty 15%
How difficult the next opponent is expected to be. A tough defence lowers the score; a weaker side raises it.
Minutes Likelihood 15%
Based on how many minutes the player has played. 90 min/game = maximum score. Rotation players who rarely complete games score lower here.
Home / Away Factor 10%
A small boost for playing at home, reflecting the well-documented home advantage in football.
Score = Form × 0.35 + Threat × 0.25 + Opponent × 0.15 + Minutes × 0.15 + Home/Away × 0.10

You can adjust the weighting of each factor on the Top Players, Hidden Gems, and Benchwarmers pages using the Prediction Controls.

Risk Levels

Each player is assigned a risk level based on their predicted score. Use this as a guide when deciding who to start.

Low Risk Score ≥ 7.0 Safe to start — strong form, regular minutes, good matchup.
Medium Risk Score 4.5 – 6.9 Proceed with caution — decent pick but some uncertainty.
High Risk Score < 4.5 Consider benching — poor form, rotation risk, or tough fixture.

Hidden Gems

Hidden Gems are undervalued players with genuine upside — typically lower-owned in fantasy leagues but projecting well.

check Predicted score between 5.5 and 7.0
check Fewer than 15 combined goals + assists — not yet on everyone's radar
check Risk level is Medium — high enough potential, low enough profile

Hidden Gems appear separately from the Top Picks list so the two groups never overlap.

Red Flags

Red Flags highlight players you should consider dropping or benching — those showing alarming dips in form and output. Only players with 270+ minutes played are assessed (so new signings aren't unfairly penalised).

Danger Score = Form Decline × 55% + Output Drop × 45%
Form Decline
How far a player's average rating has fallen below 7.0 (the "acceptable" baseline). A rating of 5.0 gives a decline score of ~4.3; below 4.0 gives a score of 4.3+.
Output Drop
Goals + Assists per 90 minutes, compared against a positional baseline:
DEF: 0.15 G+A/90 MID: 0.25 G+A/90 FWD: 0.40 G+A/90 GK: not assessed
Critical Danger Score ≥ 7.5 — drop immediately
High Danger Score 5.5 – 7.4 — seriously consider alternatives
Medium Danger Score 5.0 – 5.4 — monitor closely

Benchwarmers

Benchwarmers are consistent, reliable players who won't blow you away but won't hurt you either. Great for filling out your squad when premium options aren't available.

Consistency Score = Form Consistency × 50% + Minutes Score × 30% + Output Score × 20%
check Predicted score between 4.0 and 7.0 — not elite, but not a red flag
check Form score ≥ 5.5 and 270+ minutes played — regular, steady contributor

Match Predictions

Match outcome probabilities are powered by a machine-learning model trained on historical match data across all five leagues.

Home Win %
Probability the home side wins in 90 minutes
Draw %
Probability the match ends level after 90 minutes
Away Win %
Probability the away side wins in 90 minutes
Over 2.5 Goals %
Probability the match has 3 or more goals in total
Both Teams Score %
Probability both teams find the net at least once

Synergy

The Synergy tool lets you evaluate a group of players together rather than individually. Picking players from different positions adds a diversity bonus.

Synergy Score = Sum of Individual Scores + (Number of Distinct Positions − 1) × 0.5

A squad with all four position types (GK, DEF, MID, FWD) earns a +1.5 bonus on top of their combined scores.
