How It Works

Understanding the predictions behind every score, pick, and flag

Player Prediction Score (0 – 10)

Every player receives a predicted score representing their expected performance in the upcoming match. Internally we calculate 7 component scores (0–10) and then combine them using position-specific weights (GK/DEF/MID/FWD). In the player profile, the Prediction Controls scale the macro groups (Form/Threat/Opponent/Minutes/Home-Away) while preserving the per-position structure.

Form (scaled)
Uses the player's match rating (FormScore, 1–10). If missing, we fall back to ~6.0 as a neutral baseline.
Threat (scaled)
Combines two attacking sub-components:
• Attack (xG/90 with conversion bonus, plus shots-on-target volume)
• Creativity (xA/90 with assist bonus, plus key-pass quality)
Opponent Difficulty (scaled)
Uses the matchup difficulty (OpponentScore, 0–10). If missing we default to ~5.
Minutes Likelihood (scaled)
Uses an availability score based on minutes played, blended across season + recent matches.
Home / Away Factor (scaled)
This is the defensive component (GK save strength / DEF duels+duels volume / MID duel work / reduced FWD defensive impact).
Position-weighted PredictedScore (raw 0–10) is computed from:
GK: 0.22·Form + 0.60·Defensive + 0.06·Availability + 0.04·Discipline + 0.08·Opponent
DEF: 0.22·Form + 0.08·Attack + 0.07·Creativity + 0.30·Defensive + 0.13·Availability + 0.10·Discipline + 0.10·Opponent
MID: 0.20·Form + 0.17·Attack + 0.24·Creativity + 0.12·Defensive + 0.10·Availability + 0.09·Discipline + 0.08·Opponent
FWD: 0.18·Form + 0.33·Attack + 0.17·Creativity + 0.02·Defensive + 0.10·Availability + 0.08·Discipline + 0.12·Opponent

On the player profile page, Prediction Controls scale Form/Threat/Opponent/Minutes/Home-Away (then we renormalize). The league grids use the default controls.

Risk Levels

Each player is assigned a risk level based on their predicted score. Use this as a guide when deciding who to start.

Low Risk Score ≥ 7.0 Safe to start — strong form, regular minutes, good matchup.
Medium Risk Score 4.5 – 6.9 Proceed with caution — decent pick but some uncertainty.
High Risk Score < 4.5 Consider benching — poor form, rotation risk, or tough fixture.

Hidden Gems

Hidden Gems are undervalued players with genuine upside — typically lower-owned in fantasy leagues but projecting well.

check Predicted score between 4.5 and 8.0
check Goals+Assists below a position-typical cap (GK/DEF/MID/FWD thresholds)
check And at least one of 6 upside signals is true (xG+xA outperforming returns, shots+conversion mismatch, creativity without assists, high threat but low output, high xG/shot, or improving underlying trajectory).

Hidden Gems appear separately from the Top Picks list so the two groups never overlap.

Red Flags

Red Flags highlight players you should consider dropping or benching — those showing alarming dips in form and output. Only players with 270+ minutes played are assessed (so new signings aren't unfairly penalised).

RedFlagScore is a position-weighted composite (0–10) built from: FormDecline, OutputDrop, xThreatDecline, ShotAccuracyDecline, InvolvementDecline, DisciplineRisk (GK additionally uses gkDecline from save rate + goals conceded trends).
GK: 0.25·FormDecline + 0.45·gkDecline + 0.10·DisciplineRisk + 0.20·InvolvementDecline
DEF: 0.25·FormDecline + 0.18·OutputDrop + 0.12·xThreatDecline + 0.22·InvolvementDecline + 0.23·DisciplineRisk
MID: 0.25·FormDecline + 0.20·OutputDrop + 0.18·xThreatDecline + 0.10·ShotAccDecline + 0.17·InvolvementDecline + 0.10·DisciplineRisk
FWD: 0.25·FormDecline + 0.28·OutputDrop + 0.20·xThreatDecline + 0.15·ShotAccDecline + 0.08·InvolvementDecline + 0.04·DisciplineRisk
Form Decline
Combines an absolute drop below 7.0 (recent vs baseline) with a relative drop (overall vs recent).
Output Drop
Goals+Assists per 90 minutes, compared against a positional output baseline:
DEF: 0.10 G+A/90 MID: 0.22 G+A/90 FWD: 0.35 G+A/90 GK: not assessed
Critical Danger Score ≥ 7.5 — drop immediately
High Danger Score 5.5 – 7.4 — seriously consider alternatives
Medium Danger Score 5.0 – 5.4 — monitor closely

Benchwarmers

Benchwarmers are consistent, reliable players who won't blow you away but won't hurt you either. Great for filling out your squad when premium options aren't available.

Consistency Score is computed from position-weighted reliability components:
GK: 0.30·Availability + 0.25·FormConsistency + 0.30·OutputReliability + 0.15·Discipline
DEF: 0.25·Availability + 0.25·FormConsistency + 0.18·OutputReliability + 0.17·PassingReliability + 0.15·Discipline
MID: 0.20·Availability + 0.25·FormConsistency + 0.23·OutputReliability + 0.22·PassingReliability + 0.10·Discipline
FWD: 0.20·Availability + 0.25·FormConsistency + 0.30·OutputReliability + 0.15·PassingReliability + 0.10·Discipline
check Not a Hidden Gem and not a Red Flag (red-flag score below the cut-off)
check 270+ minutes (overall) or 45+ minutes (recent), with recent-view requiring enough recent games
check Consistency score must be at least 4.0 to produce a label
Rock SolidConsistency ≥ 7.5
Steady OptionConsistency 5.5 – 7.4

Match Predictions

Match outcome probabilities are powered by a machine-learning model trained on historical match data across all five leagues.

Home Win %
Probability the home side wins in 90 minutes
Draw %
Probability the match ends level after 90 minutes
Away Win %
Probability the away side wins in 90 minutes
Over 2.5 Goals %
Probability the match has 3 or more goals in total
Both Teams Score %
Probability both teams find the net at least once

Synergy

The Synergy tool lets you evaluate a group of players together rather than individually. Picking players from different positions adds a diversity bonus.

Synergy Score = Sum of Individual Scores + (Number of Distinct Positions − 1) × 0.5

A squad with all four position types (GK, DEF, MID, FWD) earns a +1.5 bonus on top of their combined scores.
